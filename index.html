Instruction:

    Pick your weapon
    Pick your armor
    Choose the energy of your attack
    The higher energy output of your attack, the higher chance you have of missing your mark
    if you miss you mark you do reduced damage, ex: if you choose max energy you may hit for up to 75 hp, but you could also miss or only hit for 5
    If you choose to put all of your energy into the attack, there is a chance your enemy sees the attack coming and counters your attack
    When you run out of energy you forfeit a turn
